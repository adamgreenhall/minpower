Why Minpower¶

Minpower is designed to make solving ED, OPF, and UC problems simple and intuitive. Traditional approaches to solving these problems are limited by:

  • low level languages

  • expensive software

  • being solver dependent

What’s good¶

Minpower

  • is written in Python

  • is free and open source

  • can use many solvers

  • has comparable solution times

Sounds great¶

Install Minpower and try it out.
